We're committed to making philanthropy accessible, rewarding, and impactful for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, DonorConnect was born from a simple idea: what if giving could be more engaging, transparent, and rewarding? Our founders experienced firsthand the disconnect between donors and the causes they support.

Today, we've grown into a thriving community of over 10,000 donors who have collectively contributed more than $500,000 to causes worldwide. But we're just getting started.
