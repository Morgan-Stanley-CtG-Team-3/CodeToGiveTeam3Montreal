Shield of Athena

For over three decades, Shield of Athena has been a beacon of hope for families escaping violence. We provide emergency shelter, counseling services, and prevention programs that break the cycle of abuse.

Our comprehensive approach addresses immediate safety needs while building long-term resilience through therapy, education, and community support. Every donation directly funds life-saving services and prevention programs.
